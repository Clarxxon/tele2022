IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) is the largest international organization dedicated to promoting innovation in electrical, electronic, computing and related fields of science and technology, headquartered in the United States in New Jersey.

Most of the IEEE members are electrical and electronic engineers, but the wide range of interests attracts people in other industries, such as computer science, software engineering, mechanical engineering, civil engineering, biology, physics, mathematics, etc.

IEEE is the main publisher of scientific journals and organizer of conferences, seminars, symposia. It is also a leading standards development organization with over 900 active industry technical standards across a wide range of disciplines, including electricity, biomedical technology and healthcare, information technology, telecommunications, transportation, space, and nanotechnology.

The webinar is intended for acquaintance only with new or future members of IEEE from. The event will address the following issues and topics:

  • What are the benefits of membership in the IEEE for its participants?
  • What scientific communities currently exist in the IEEE?
  • How to become a member of the IEEE organization and join the scientific community?
